Privacy

Privacy policy

Effective June 8, 2026
The short version
Leludo runs on your device. We never sell or share your data. We use anonymous Google Analytics to count screens viewed and game events — no names, no save state, no individual moves. No accounts, no advertising, no other third-party trackers.
What stays on your device
The game uses your browser’s local storage to remember:
  • Theme choice (Paper / Dusk / System)
  • Assist toggles (auto-roll, auto-move)
  • Bot name pool preference
  • Names you’ve typed for seats
  • Your in-progress saved game

None of this ever leaves your device.

Anonymous analytics
We use Google Analytics 4 to understand which screens get used, whether games start and finish, and roughly where the build is running (Play Store APK, the leludo.org website, an installed PWA, or an embed on a portal like itch.io). This helps us decide what to improve next.

What gets sent:

  • Screen names (home, board, settings, pause, etc.)
  • Generic game events (game start, game end, capture, store-nudge click)
  • App version
  • Platform & source (android, web, itch, pwa, …)
  • Browser / device metadata supplied automatically by Google Analytics, including IP-derived approximate location (typically country)

What is never sent:

  • Player names you type for seats
  • Your saved game, the board state, or any individual move you make
  • Any value that could identify you personally

Cookie: Google Analytics sets a _ga cookie to count distinct visitors. No advertising cookies.

Opt out: install the Google Analytics opt-out add-on, enable your browser’s tracking-prevention or Do-Not-Track setting, or use a tracker-blocking extension. Analytics is also disabled during local development.

What we don’t do
No advertising SDKs. No fingerprinting. No cross-site tracking. No remote servers receive your seat names, save state, or individual moves. The only third-party service we contact is Google Analytics, scoped as described above.
External links
The home screen links to the project’s GitHub repository. Following that link takes you to github.com, which has its own privacy policy. We have no insight into what GitHub does with that visit.
Android app
The Android build is the same app packaged with Capacitor. It requests no special permissions and makes no network calls beyond the anonymous Google Analytics events described above and links you tap yourself.
Clearing your data
In a browser: clear site data for this domain in your browser settings. On Android: clear app storage in system settings, or uninstall the app. Either action wipes every preference and saved game listed above.
Children
The game is suitable for all ages. Because we collect nothing, there is nothing child-specific to disclose under COPPA, GDPR-K, or similar regimes.
Changes
If this policy ever changes, the effective date at the top of this page updates with it. Material changes will be called out in the project changelog on GitHub.
Contact
Questions or concerns: open an issue at github.com/LeludoOrg/leludo.
